About Me

Hey, I’m Edmond! I am an aspiring computer science student starting my senior year at Agincourt Collegiate Institute.

Like many of my peers, I have always been exposed to different mediums of technology. From simple iOS applications to complex virtual reality simulators, these intricate digital creations have never failed to amaze me. As I grew older, I naturally became compelled to the science behind these applications. This is where my interest in coding began. As I continue to expand my knowledge in this field, I hope to showcase my progression through projects and tackling various problem sets.

Beyond that, I love spending my spare time with my family and playing video games with my friends. I also enjoy allocating time for my hobbies, like speedcubing.
